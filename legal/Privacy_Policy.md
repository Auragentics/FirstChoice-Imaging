## 1. DEFINITIONS

**"Client"** means any business entity that has entered into a service agreement with Auragentics for the provision of AI voice agent, A2P SMS messaging, or related services.

**"Client Data"** means all data, information, and content that a Client or its end users provide, transmit, or make available through the Services, including but not limited to call recordings, transcripts, caller logs, and usage statistics.

**"End User"** means any individual who interacts with an AI voice agent or messaging service operated by Auragentics on behalf of a Client.

**"Personal Information"** means any information that identifies, relates to, describes, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular individual or household.

**"Protected Health Information" or "PHI"** has the meaning ascribed to it under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 ("HIPAA"), as amended.

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 2.1 Information Collected from Platform Users (Client Dashboard Users)

When you register for or use the Platform, we may collect:

- **Account Information:** Full name, email address, job title, company name, phone number, and login credentials.
- **Usage Data:** Log-in timestamps, pages viewed, features accessed, session duration, IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and operating system.
- **Communication Data:** Support requests, feedback, and correspondence with Auragentics.

### 2.2 Information Collected Through AI Voice Agent Services

In the course of providing AI voice agent services on behalf of our Clients, the following information may be collected from End Users:

- **Caller Information:** Phone numbers, names, dates of birth, email addresses, and other identifying information voluntarily provided during a call.
- **Call Data:** Voice recordings, call transcripts, call duration, call timestamps, caller intent classifications, and call disposition data.
- **Request Information:** Nature of inquiry, service requests, scheduling preferences, and other information provided during the interaction.
- **SMS Data:** Phone numbers, message content, delivery status, opt-in/opt-out status, and timestamps.

### 2.3 Information Collected Automatically

- **Log Data:** Server logs, error reports, and diagnostic data.
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated and anonymized usage statistics, performance metrics, and system health data.
- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** We may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance Platform functionality, analyze usage patterns, and improve our Services. You may control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to: (a) comply with applicable law; (b) protect our rights or property; (c) prevent fraud or abuse; or (d) protect the safety of any person.
