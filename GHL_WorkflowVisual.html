📋 Workflow Summary

  • Trigger: Fires when voice agent sends POST webhook after call ends
  • Contact: Creates or updates GHL contact with caller data
  • Routing: Uses intents_handled field with contains-based routing to support multi-intent calls
  • Actions: Sends staff email, caller SMS, and applies tracking tag

⚠️ Critical: intents_handled Field

The intents_handled field must be passed from your voice agent and mapped to the GHL custom field. This is a comma-separated list for multi-intent calls. The If/Else condition uses a "contains" check to route to the correct email template.

Valid values: records, billing, lien (comma-separated for multi-intent)
